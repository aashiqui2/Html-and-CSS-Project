The story of Naruto is about Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who wants recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The story is told in two parts: volumes 1–27 are set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and volumes 28–72 are set in his teens.Naruto has a sad upbringing. He grows up alone, believing that no one cares or even acknowledges him. However, he eventually has people come into his life and is able to walk down the right path.
